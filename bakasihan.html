ENTOY'S BAKASIHAN



Entoy's Bakasihan is probably the oldest and most famous restaurant serving bakasi in town as the area is abundant with reef eels because the sea's tidal flats are mostly grassy and muddy which are conducive to the eel's sustenance. So if you're ready to try out something extraordinary with your friends and family, have a stopover here and enjoy the cool breeze of the sea while savoring the infamous Bakasi.
